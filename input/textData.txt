Generally, any device that can perform numerical calculations, even an adding machine,
may be called a computer but nowadays this term is used especially for digital computers.
Computers that once weighed 30 tons now may weigh as little as 1.8 kilograms.
Microchips and microprocessors have considerably reduced the cost of the electronic components required
in a computer. Computers come in many sizes and shapes such as special-purpose, laptop, desktop,
minicomputers, supercomputers.
